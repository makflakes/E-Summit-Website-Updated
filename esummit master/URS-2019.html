E-Summit 2020


The Entrepreneurhsip and Innovation Cell of Mahindra Ecole Centrale welcomes you to its first E-SUMMIT.  Scheduled for January 24th, 2020 the event will lay host to a variety of unique events such as the Startup Sprint in addition to various standard events such as Startup Showcase and Business Idea Competition. Entrepreneurs and Business leaders from all over India will be joining us to deliver keynote speeches, take part in panel discussions and interactions with the students.
