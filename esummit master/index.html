E-Summit 2020


The Entrepreneurship and Innovation Cell of Mahindra Ecole Centrale welcomes you to its first E-SUMMIT.  Scheduled for the 24th and 25th of January, 2020 the event will play host to a variety of unique events such as the Startup Sprint in addition to various standard events such as Startup Showcase and Business Idea Competition. Students and participants get an opportunity to attend keynote speeches, panel discussions and interact with successful entrepreneurs, mentors and investors.
