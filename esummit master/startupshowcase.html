Startup Showcase


Are you part of a startup ? Want to showcase it, validate it and test market it. E-Summit 2020 provides a platform for already established or prototype stage startups to showcase their product/service/prototype and validate their ideas with potential customers/ mentors and investors in addition to winning handsome prizes.

Guidelines
  • The competition will be held on 25th January, 2020
  • The startup should solve one of the UN Sustainable Development goals either specifically or vaguely.
  • The interested startups must submit their application on or before 1st January, 2020

  • A short abstract of not more than 300 words about your startup must be submitted at the time of registration
  • The participants should be registered as a company and should come ready with their product/service/prototype to showcase.
  • The participants should talk about the motivation behind their startup, the process involved in building it up and future aspirations.

  • The top 3 startups will be awarded prizes and will be given the oppurtunity to be mentored and incubated with Mahindra E-Hub.

E-Summit 2020

The Entrepreneurhsip and Innovation Cell of Mahindra Ecole Centrale welcomes you to its first E-SUMMIT. Scheduled for January 24th, 2020 the event will lay host to a variety of unique events such as the Startup Sprint in addition to various standard events such as Startup Showcase and Business Idea Competition. Entrepreneurs and Business leaders from all over India will be joining us to deliver keynote speeches, take part in panel discussions and interactions with the students.
